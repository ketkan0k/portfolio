Who I Am

The Story
Behind the Work

I'm a Product Owner and Project Manager with over 5 years of experience leading end-to-end digital product development in the real estate industry at Noble Development PCL — one of Thailand's leading property developers.

My work spans the full product lifecycle: translating C-Level business direction into structured development plans, hands-on UX/UI review in Figma, QA oversight, UAT coordination, user training, and post-launch support.

I currently own and manage 8+ digital platforms — POS (CRM, Booking, Contract, Transfer) and Non-POS (Noble ID, Website, CMS, CDP, Payment) — serving as the company's sole Product Owner for its entire digital ecosystem.

Before stepping into product roles, I spent 3 years in Japan as a Manufacturing Operations Coordinator at OMRON Corporation — an experience that sharpened my precision, process discipline, and cross-cultural communication.
